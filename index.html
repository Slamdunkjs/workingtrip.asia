Do you need a visa?

Work visas

  • Ideal for work and tourism.
  • Allows you to stay in Europe for 365/365 days.
  • The embassy will issue you a “one-year” type D / multiple-entry visa, and it can be renewed every year or applied for PR on certain grounds.
  • Processing Time: 1-2 months.

Student Visas

  • Ideal for training, work and tourism.
  • The age for processing is from 15 to 35 years.
  • Allows you to stay in Europe for 6-12 months.
  • A national visa of type D11 is a standard educational visa with the right to work. Issued to foreigners intending to attend university studies.
  • Processing time: 1 month.

Business visa

  • Ideal for business and tourism.
  • Allows you to stay in Europe for 90/180 days.
  • Business visa (С04) - this type of visa is issued to businessmen who need it to make frequent business trips.
  • Processing time: 1 month.
